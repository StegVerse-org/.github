Every system action crosses a boundary: constraints collapse, reality is touched, something irreversible happens.

Most systems log what they observed.

This does something different.

—> It captures what was knowable, what was recovered,  and what was lost - with cryptographic receipts for every state transition. <—


The shaded regions in the sketch?

That's where observability breaks down.

The demo doesn't ignore them, it proves, measures, and receipts each one.
• Full playback;
• Full reconstruction;
• Integrity check against the final state.

If reconstruction doesn't match reality, you don't guess. You know exactly where the unrecovered information lives.
